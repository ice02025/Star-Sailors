BEGINNER GUIDE

Beginner Guide

Start from scratch, master the core gameplay of Star Sailors, and quickly grow into a capable starship captain

STEP BY STEP

First Voyage Guide

Follow the steps below to quickly learn the basics of Star Sailors

1

Enter the Game & Character Creation

After launching the game, tap the screen to enter the main menu. On your first playthrough, you can customize your character's appearance including hairstyle, hair color, and facial features. Confirm to officially begin your interstellar voyage.

2

Watch the Opening Story

After character creation, the game plays an opening cinematic introducing the world and the protagonist's amnesia. To skip and jump into the action, tap the "Skip" button in the top-right corner. The first battle begins automatically after the cutscene.

3

Select Battle Skills

Before battle, tap the "+" icon next to each character's portrait to select the skill to use this turn. Different skills have different effect ranges and costs. Some advanced skills require SP to cast, so plan your resources wisely.

4

Execute Battle

Once all characters have their skills assigned, tap the "Battle" button below to start. Characters will execute their actions automatically based on your commands. Watch for QTE prompts on screen -- seize the burst opportunity for massive damage!

5

Return to Main Menu

After battle, you return to the main menu where you can check character levels, stamina, gold, and current event info. Tap "Battle" to continue the main story, or take on side stages for more rewards and experience.

6

Companion Leveling & Upgrades

Tap "Upgrade" → "Partner" in the bottom menu to strengthen your companions. Allocate resources wisely, focus on core characters, and boost your overall combat power.

7

View Story Content

Tap the "Story" button to view the main storyline, daily quest narratives, and companion-exclusive stories. Dive deep into character backgrounds and world lore. Completing certain companion stories unlocks special rewards.

GAME MODES

Game Modes Explained

Understand the different game modes and plan your daily playtime efficiently

Main Story

Core content that pushes the game forward. Follow the legendary compass to uncover the secrets of the floating continents. Chapter 1 is fully available with over 70 battle stages. Completing the main story unlocks new companions, areas, and features.

Daily Quests

Six companion-exclusive story quests refresh daily. Completing them yields generous amounts of EXP, gold, and upgrade materials. Prioritize these as they are one of the main sources of daily resources.

Dungeon

Randomly generated dungeon instances where monster distribution and treasure locations vary each run. High risk, high reward -- an important source of rare equipment and materials. Group up to challenge higher difficulties.

Raid

Cooperative mode where you team up with other players to challenge high-difficulty bosses. Requires proper team composition and coordination. Raid-exclusive gear drops far surpass regular equipment.

Arena

Real-time PvP mode against other players. Adapt your tactics based on opponent lineups, testing your character knowledge and team-building depth. Higher ranks yield greater rewards.

Event

Time-limited special event stages, often tied to new character releases or holiday themes. Completing events grants limited companions, skins, and exclusive items. Prioritize these whenever available.

PRO TIPS

Essential Tips for Beginners

Practical advice from veteran captains to help you avoid common pitfalls

Prioritize Daily Quests

Daily quests are the most stable source of EXP and materials during the early game. Log in every day and complete them first. Finishing all 6 daily storylines provides substantial upgrade resources.

Focus on Core Characters

Resources are limited early on -- do not spread them evenly across all companions. Pick 2-3 core characters and invest heavily in them to ensure smooth main story progression. Prioritize higher-rated characters among the 10 free companions.

Master QTE Timing

QTE burst opportunities during battle are the key to victory. Tap the screen quickly when the prompt appears. Successful QTE triggers deal extra damage or special effects -- especially critical in boss fights.

Understand Elemental Counters

The game features five elements: Fire, Water, Wind, Light, and Dark, with counter relationships between them. When building your team, diversify elements to exploit enemy weaknesses. Counter-attacking deals bonus damage.

Manage Stamina Wisely

Stamina is the key resource gating your progression. Prioritize spending stamina on main story and daily quests. During events, consider purchasing stamina refills when needed.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there an English version?

Yes! The game supports Korean and English. The interface is intuitive, and this guide will help you get started smoothly.

How do I get companions for free?

The game offers 10 free companions obtainable through main story progression, daily quests, and events. No payment required.

What if the game lags?

Try lowering graphics settings, closing background apps, or clearing your phone's cache. If using an emulator, allocate more RAM and CPU resources.

How do I earn SP?

SP is accumulated during battle through normal attacks and specific skills. Some companion skills can also restore SP for the team. Managing SP effectively is key to late-game combat.
