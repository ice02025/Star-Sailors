COMBAT SYSTEM

Combat System Overview

A comprehensive look at Star Sailors' unique combat mechanics

Star Sailors' combat system combines the strategic depth of traditional turn-based RPGs with the speed-driven excitement of QTE action elements. In every battle, you must select skills for each companion, balance SP allocation, consider elemental counter relationships, and anticipate boss attack patterns. At critical moments, QTE burst prompts appear on screen -- tap quickly to trigger massive damage or special effects, a game-changing mechanic that can turn the tide of battle.

SP MANAGEMENT

SP Management

Allocate skill resources wisely so every action counts

SP (Skill Points) is the essential resource for casting advanced skills in battle. Each battle starts with a fixed amount of SP, and certain companions can restore SP through normal attacks or specific abilities. Low-tier skills cost 1-2 SP, while high-tier skills may require 3-5 SP. Planning your SP usage sequence is the core of battle strategy -- it is recommended to conserve enough SP before boss fights to ensure your ultimate skills are ready at critical moments.

TEAM BUILDING

Team Composition Recommendations

Choose the optimal lineup for each stage -- three classic setups to help you clear content with ease

Balanced Team

Recommended: 1 Tank + 1 Healer + 2 DPS. Ideal for most stages with a solid mix of offense and defense. Suggested: Mui (Tank) + Dora (Healer) + Nina (DPS) + Princess Jen (DPS)

Blitz Team

Recommended: 3 DPS + 1 Support. Best for fast clears and easy stages, maximizing burst damage. Suggested: Nina + Kai + Luna (DPS) + Sera (Speed Support)

Defensive Counter Team

Recommended: 2 Tanks + 1 Healer + 1 DPS. Built for high-difficulty boss fights, prioritizing survival and control. Suggested: Mui + Raven (Tanks) + Dora (Healer) + Princess Jen (DPS)

BOSS STRATEGY

Boss Battle Tactics

No more helplessness against powerful bosses -- master these tips for easy victories

Observe Boss Attack Patterns

Bosses typically follow fixed skill rotations. After observing 2-3 rounds, you can predict their next move and prepare defenses or interrupts in advance.

Save QTE for Critical Skills

Don't rush to use QTE -- trigger the burst right before a boss unleashes its ultimate skill to interrupt and negate the deadly attack.

Switch Targets Flexibly

Boss fights often feature summoned minions. Prioritize clearing adds to reduce team pressure, then focus fire on the boss.

Prepare Backup Members

For high-difficulty content, have 2-3 substitute characters ready to swap in. Switch to counter-element DPS based on the boss's element.
