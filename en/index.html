About Star Sailors

Star Sailors is a Japanese fantasy-style turn-based RPG mobile game developed by renowned Korean studio Com2uS Holdings. Players take on the role of an amnesiac deep-space explorer, piloting a starship across different galaxies.

  • Platform: Android / iOS
  • Genre: Turn-Based RPG / Card Collection
  • Languages: Korean / English
  • Version: v0.5.11 (Actively Updated)
  • Size: Approx. 380MB
  • Highlights: QTE Burst Mechanic / Elemental Reactions / Summon System
