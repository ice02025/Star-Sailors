ELEMENT SYSTEM

Elemental Counter Chart

Master the strengths and weaknesses of the five elements to deal bonus damage against enemy vulnerabilities

Attack Element Strong Against (1.5x DMG) Weak Against (0.75x DMG)
Fire Wind Water
Water Fire Wind
Wind Water Fire
Light Dark (mutual 1.5x) Dark (mutual 1.5x)
Dark Light (mutual 1.5x) Light (mutual 1.5x)

Counter Rules:
Fire → Wind → Water → Fire forms a three-element rotation counter chain. Light and Dark mutually counter each other -- both deal bonus damage (1.5x) when attacking one another. When building a team, aim to cover at least 2-3 elements to handle different enemy weaknesses. Damage is reduced to 0.75x when countered -- avoid using disadvantaged elements against enemies whenever possible.
