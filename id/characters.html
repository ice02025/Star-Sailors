ELEMENT SYSTEM

Tabel Elemental Counter

Kuasai hubungan counter antara lima elemen. Serang weakness musuh buat dapet damage bonus!

Elemen Serangan Kuat Terhadap (Damage x1.5) Lemah Terhadap (Damage x0.75)
Fire Wind Water
Water Fire Wind
Wind Water Fire
Light Dark (saling counter, damage x1.5) Dark (saling counter, damage x1.5)
Dark Light (saling counter, damage x1.5) Light (saling counter, damage x1.5)

Aturan Elemental Counter:
Fire → Wind → Water → Fire membentuk rantai counter tiga elemen. Light dan Dark saling counter -- masing-masing ngasih damage bonus (x1.5) saat menyerang satu sama lain. Saat build tim, usahakan cover minimal 2-3 elemen biar siap hadapi berbagai weakness musuh. Kalau elemen kamu kena counter, damage turun jadi 0.75x -- hindari pakai elemen yang lemah lawan musuh.
